Conor and Rachael's wedding celebration

2nd August 2025

What's the Craic?

In a Galaxy far far away...

We got hitched back in 2020 during the height of COVID, a small but mighty party was had, masks, social distancing and all. But big plans were put on hold, kinda forgotten about for a bit, and now reshaped and re-released.

So finally it's time to celebrate properly with all our family and friends!

What's Happening?

It's going to be a festival-style celebration at our farm in Leitir. We've got a massive tipi marquee that'll fit over 100 people, a caterer is going to do some fancy BBQ-style food, a bit of music, bit of craic and a bit of a mad plan to turn a barn into a bar.

Getting Here

Public Transport

Flying

To Carrickfin Airport

This could be a good option for some. It's a 45min flight from Dublin, Terminal 2 (the nice one), fast tracked security line and of course one of the most senic airports in the world. On a clear day it really is spectacular. Flights are usualy €30 each way but if it gets very windy they can be cancelled. August should be fine though.

Bus

Be warned, it's a long slog. €15 each way

Bus Éireann Route 30 gets you to Donegal Town

Route 32 gets you to Letterkenny

Add about 30mins to advertised timetable so each route takes approx 4.5 hours. If you get that far we'll collect and drive you the rest of the way.

Directions

Driving

From Dublin

4.5 hours

We all have our own preference but I think the N3 to Cavan then head toward Enniskillen. If you have a satnav or Google Maps then going by Blacklion/Belco takes 20mins off and is very senic too.

From Galway

3.5 hours

There's only one road really. N17 -> Sligo -> Ballyshannon -> Donegal Town -> Glenties -> Leitir
