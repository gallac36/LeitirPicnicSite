Getting to Leitir

aka Lettermacaward

Presuming that we haven't built a wall around aul Donegal to keep the Corona out.


Driving

From Dublin

4.5 hours

We all have our own preference but I think the N3 to Cavan then head toward Enniskillen. If you have a satnav or Google Maps then going by Blacklion/Belco takes 20mins off and is pretty senic too.

From Galway

3.5 hours

There's only one road really. N17 -> Sligo -> Ballyshannon -> Donegal Town -> Glenties -> Leitir

Flying

To Carrickfin Airport

This could be a good option for some. It's a 45min flight from Dublin, Terminal 2 (the nice one), fast tracked security line and of course one of the most senic airports in the world. On a clear day it really is spectacular. Flights are usualy €30 each way but if it gets very windy they can be cancelled. September should be fine though. Shouldn't it?

Bus

It's a long slog, and it ain't even particularly cheap but it is good for the enviroment I suppose. €15 each way

Bus Éireann Route 30 gets you to Donegal Town

Route 32 gets you to Letterkenny

Add about 30mins to advertised timetable so each route takes approx 4.5 hours. If you get that far we'll collect and drive you the rest of the way.
